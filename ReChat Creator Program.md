# Introducing: The ReChat Creator Program

We are thrilled to officially announce the **ReChat Creator Program**, our way of supporting and celebrating the amazing content creators within our community.

### What is the ReChat Creator Program?

This exclusive program is designed to reward eligible YouTube creators with enhanced tools, direct access to our team, and special recognition for their contributions.

### Exclusive Member Benefits

As a member of the ReChat Creator Program, you will gain access to:

* **Official YouTube CC Role:** Stand out in our community with a verified role.
* **ReChat Music License:** Gain access to our library of licensed music for your content.
* **Priority Support:** Receive direct attention for all bug reports and feature suggestions.
* **Custom Referral Links:** Track your impact with personalized links tailored to your channel.
* **...and much more!**
